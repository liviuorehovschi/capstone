Background & Significance

Lung cancer diagnosis relies heavily on manual interpretation of histopathological images, which is time-intensive and subject to variability among pathologists. This project seeks to address these challenges by combining advanced AI methods with practical applications to enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.

Clinical Impact

Providing a faster, consistent diagnostic tool supports pathologists and expedites treatment decisions, potentially improving patient outcomes.

Research Applications

The system facilitates analysis of large histopathological datasets, contributing to research in cancer detection and treatment development.

Educational Value

The tool offers medical students an accessible platform to explore histopathological patterns, enhancing their diagnostic training.

Challenges in Diagnosis

Traditional diagnostic methods face several issues:

  • Labor-intensive processes prone to variability
  • Limited availability of expert pathologists
  • Increased workload with growing case volumes

Future Directions

To expand the utility of this tool, we plan to:

  • Integrate additional cancer types and diagnostic tasks
  • Implement explainable AI features for better interpretability
  • Transition the application to cloud-based platforms for scalability
  • Collaborate with medical institutions for clinical validation
